    For service fees paid in advance, we offer a full refund if canceled within 48 hours of payment, and a prorated refund based on services already rendered if canceled later. Registration fees for property searches or listings qualify for a 100% refund if canceled before any services are provided, and partial refunds if minimal work has been performed.
